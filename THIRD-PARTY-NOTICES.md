## Sounds

`miri_fallback_alarm.wav` is a synthesized tone, not a sampled recording, and
carries no third-party rights. Its structure is machine-generated throughout: a
1046.5 Hz (C6) band-limited square wave — odd harmonics only — in three 150 ms
beeps on an exact 250 ms period, with 8 ms linear fades, digital silence between
beeps, and no encoder metadata.
